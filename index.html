    • Announcement: Irish Festival

    Saturday March 3rd, The Worcester County Firefighter's Emerald Society will be hosting the 9th annual Irish Festival. The Festival starts at 5:00pm. Buy tickets Here.

    Days until the 9th Annual Irish Festival! Have you purchased your tickets yet?

    We are proudly donating this year’s proceeds to On-Site Academy !

    The entertainment will be provided by Boston’s Erin Og, The Green Rovers, Anne O’Connell’s Irish-American Step Dancers and Pipes & Drums from around the Commonwealth.

    Enjoy a variety of traditional Irish fare, expertly created by Sean Patrick's Restaurant. Sample some local farm delights, including baked goods from Stillman Dairy Farm and a selection of fine cheeses from Smith's Country Cheese .

    Of course you’ll want to wash all of this down with one of Wachusett Brewing Company’s finest brews. For the second year in a row, we have teamed up with Wachusett to bring you this lively event that just keeps growing!

    Buy your tickets today! https://irishfestival.ticketbud.com/2018

    What: 9th Annual Irish Festival

    When: Saturday March, 3rd, 2018. Time: 5:00pm - 10:00pm

    Location: O'Neill Hall at The Fitchburg Armory, 14 Wallace Ave, Fitchburg, MA 01420
