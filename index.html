    Irish Festival

    Now that the dust has settled, we'd like to take a few minutes to thank everyone who made our 9th Annual Irish Festival such a success! Meticulous planning and hard work go a long way and it showed. Thank you to all of the Emerald Society volunteers! Thanks so much to our supporters and sponsors - Mayor Stephen DiNatale, Fitchburg, bankHometown, The Monument Tap, The Blacksheep Tavern, Chapdelaine Buick GMC and Wachusett Brewing Company! A huge thank you to our vendors who made the night so special - Sean Patrick's Restaurant, Smith's Country Cheese, Stillman Dairy Farm, Tesla Solar Energy, Boston Fire Gear. Thanks to the amazing entertainment this year, from the step dancers to Boston Erin Og, The Green Rovers and the very talented Westford & District Pipes and Drums and Police Pipes & Drums of Worcester, Massachusetts. We look forward to welcoming you all back next year!

    Most of all, thank you to everyone that came and celebrated with us, bought a ticket, made a donation, purchased a raffle ticket or just wished us good cheer! Our sold out event allowed us to donate more funds than we've ever been able to and we're so very honored to be helping On-Site Academy, an organization that has helped so many of us and those we love!

    Our 10th Annual event promises to be bigger and better, with a few surprises up our sleeve! We love all of our supporters and are so proud to host what has become such a cultural mainstay for North Central Massachusetts and the City of Fitchburg! Be sure to check out the photo album from this year's event, courtesy of our exceptionally dedicated Secretary Raquel Jandrow Howard.

    Happy St. Patrick's Day to all!
