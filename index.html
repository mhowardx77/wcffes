    Spaghetti Supper

    Want to help victims of Hurricane Harvey and Hurricane Irma? Join the Worcester County Firefighters' Emerald Society as we present a night of delicious meals and great raffles with 100% of the proceeds going directly to First Responders in Texas, Louisiana, and Florida who have lost everything. Firefighters, Police Officers and EMS crews have been working non-stop around the clock to help save lives and property while many of their own properties lay in ruin.

    How do we know it's going to get to them? We're hand delivering the proceeds when we travel to Florida for our relief and recovery effort on September 26.

    Email our executive board if you care to make a donation in the way of funds or a raffle prize. wcffes@gmail.com

    What: Spaghetti Supper

    When: Thursday, September 21 from 4:00 PM - 8:00 PM EDT

    Location: Knights of Columbus - 484 Lancaster St, Leominster, Massachusetts 01453
